Maze Generator

I have created an impeccable maze generator utilizing the Hunt-and-Kill method. The code is designed with modularity in mind, allowing for seamless integration of additional maze generation algorithms. This generator is capable of producing mazes ranging from 10x10 to 250x250 in size, ensuring that the entire maze is always visible to the user. Furthermore, it adapts flawlessly to different screen resolutions, ensuring a smooth experience. While the visual aesthetics may not be the most striking, this project serves as a testament to my programming skills and expertise.
